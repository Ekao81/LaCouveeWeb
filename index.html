"Annaej Ash - Goalie Parents - all access - FB-group

Hey Goalies and Goalie Parents, happy Saturday. Iam Joanna, goalie mom to an 11yr old goalie who plays Rep AA in Woodstock, ON Canada. This morning we had our first virtual meeting with Connor! I wanted to share our experience in case any of you were thinking about doing the 30 minute virtual sessions and had some reservations that were holding you back!

My son is a fairly shy kiddo and has had some very harsh and negative coaching personalities in the past. As a result, he was quite nervous to meet Connor, not knowing what to expect. In his Words, "Connor was easy to talk to and fun When he was asked what the biggest take away from his time with Connor was, ( what he liked most) he said, the positive affirmations. Connor helped him choose 3 positive affirming statements to focus on and repeat to prepare his mindset for a game or practice.

As my son gets older I have noticed how the mental aspect of the game is changing for him and there has been no support for him. As a Children's Mental Health professional for over 16 years, I worry about the impact the world of hockey can have on our youth without the right people supporting and guiding them! Connor is such a positive, fun and inspiring human and I really respect how he gives so much special attention to the mental aspect of the game along side the technical components. In the Zoom platform, Connor was able to share his screen with us, review some video footage, provide feedback on his positioning, and then got up to demonstrate what errors my son made and then showed him the Correction! We will definitely continue to work with Connor to both improve the technical aspect mental fortitude! Thanks Connor for a great first session. Will of his game meanwhile strengthening his be in touch soon for Session #2.
